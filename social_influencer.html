Influencer Marketing

marketing budget Trends

 Over $2B was spent on influencer marketing programs in 2017. 48% of Marketers surveyed stated their Influencer Marketing budgets will grow this year. In 2017, brands spent an average of $75K on individual influencers with at least 500K followers - up 48% from the previous year

browsers are better at blocking ads

70% of web users use ad blockers, making traditional web advertising less effective - brands are diverting spend to influencer marketing programs

not all platforms are equal

Not all brands view (and invest) all social media platforms equally. Instagram dominates the space with almost 1/3 of all influencer marketing investments
